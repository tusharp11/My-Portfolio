About The Engineer

I'm an Aeronautical Engineer specialising in the full lifecycle of UAV systems — from initial concept and R&D through to DGCA Type Certification and operational deployment.

My expertise spans avionics integration, propulsion and sensor systems, flight controller firmware tuning (ArduPilot / PX4), ground control station development, and flight test campaigns with rigorous telemetry analysis.

At Marut Dronetech I lead multi-disciplinary certification projects while contributing to novel patent ideation for UAV subsystems. Previously at Chatak Innovations I grew from integrating operational UAVs to leading the firmware department and entire type certification programmes.

I hold a B.Tech in Aeronautical Engineering (Grade A) from ADCET, and a DGCA Medium Class Remote Pilot licence.
